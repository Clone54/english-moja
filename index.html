Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learing English on this website?

+
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

Is this website free to use?

+
Yes! All our lessons, vocabulary lists, and word details are completely free to access. Our goal is to make language learning accessible to everyone without any subscription fees.

Do I need to create an account?

+
No, you don't need to create an account to access our lessons and vocabulary lists. You can start learning right away without any registration.

How can I build my English vocabulary?

+
The best way is to learn words in context. We recommend following our structured Lessons, which group words by difficulty and category. After learning a word's meaning, try using it in a sentence, listen to its pronunciation, and check its synonyms to see how it relates to words you already know. Consistency is key—try learning 5 to 10 new words every day!

Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

+
No, we do not currently offer certificates for completed courses. However, you can track your progress and review your learning materials anytime through our platform.
